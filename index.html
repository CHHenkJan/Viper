SIGNAL METHODOLOGY

No signal fires without all three.

01 TREND

EMA Direction

02 REVERSAL

Stochastic Trigger

Stochastic(5,3,3) must be below 20 and crossing back up — oversold reversal, not chasing tops.

%K < 20 · %K ↗ %D
03 CONFIRMATION

Volume Validation

Current volume must exceed the 20-period average. No volume = no conviction = no trade.

V₀ > ⌀V₂₀

All three conditions must be true simultaneously. If one is missing, the signal is suppressed. Strict filtering reduces noise at the cost of frequency — and we're fine with that. Quality over quantity.
